### Featured Research & Projects

#### [GenomeBERT](https://github.com/qunamab/genomebert)
*Translating NLP innovations into the language of life.*
A lightweight Transformer model that treats DNA sequences as text to predict functional regulatory regions like promoters.

#### [RAG vs Long-Context (rag_vs_lc)](https://github.com/qunamab/rag_vs_lc)
*When Context Overwhelms: Long-Context vs. Retrieval-Based QA Under Noise.*
Co-evaluated the **K2-Think-v2 (671B)** model across 7,160+ trials, identifying a novel **"over-reasoning" failure mode** in large-scale reasoning architectures.
> **DOI**: [10.5281/ZENODO.19693922](https://doi.org/10.5281/ZENODO.19693922)

#### [Neuro-Ranker (brain)](https://github.com/qunamab/brain)
*High-Fidelity Neural Simulation Engine.*
Predicts human brain fMRI BOLD responses to multimodal stimuli using a deterministic synthetic engine.
